About Game

Initially released in 2017, PUBG has significantly influenced the battle royale genre, establishing many of the conventions and mechanics that have become standard in the genre. Available on multiple platforms, including PC, consoles, and mobile devices, PUBG offers a thrilling and competitive multiplayer experience.PUBGâ€™s gameplay is intense and strategic.
